Welcome to What2Take!

Are you looking for the perfect class to take with friends during your last year of University? Or maybe an easy elective to get that GPA up? What about an all around awesome class with a great professor?

Look no further! What2Take provides you with reviews and other very useful information to aid in your decision process. The best part is the information is user generated by you, the students!

About Us

Our names (from top left on) are Chris, Elie, Josh, Nik, Josh, and Elias. We are fourth year students in the Business Technology Management specialization at the Telfer School of Management. We were brought together on this project for Management and Implementation of Web Technologies in Organizations.

Our inspiration came from our constant search for electives out of our faculty that we need to graduate. As opposed to scouring the internet for course syllabuses for these courses, we wanted to create a one-stop shop to find the perfect elective for you. That is how What2Take was born!
